How to set up Google Analytics

To get started with Google Analytics, head to the Google Analytics login page. When logging in with your Google account, the page will guide you through the needed settings. You will need to insert an account name of your choice as well as choose wether you want to track a website, mobile app or both. On the third step you have to type in the name of your website, website’s domain (e.g. arnhembusinessschool.com) and other information about your website. You can follow the steps with our Google Analytics tutorial below.

Google Analytics training

After setting up your Google Analytics you’re good to go! How? There’s definitely a lot of help available for Google Analytics beginners. For starters, we recommend you to check out the official Google Analytics training here. After finishing up with this training you will receive a Google Analytics certification to prove your knowledge. There are also a lot of other Google Analytics trainings available online, as well as real life seminars with guidance. There is also an official Google Analytics demo account provided if you want to try it out!

How to add Google Analytics to WordPress?

If you’re up for getting more technical, Google Analytics is ready! As an example, adding Google Analytics to WordPress is possible by following the steps provided by WPBeginner. There are also many integrations available for different kinds of needs. Would you like to see your Google Ads reports combined together with your Google Analytics data? Or how about being able to visualize your data exactly how you prefer with Data Studio? No problem - Google Analytics integrations provides it all! There is also a Google Analytics API available for developers.
