WHAT IS GOOGLE ANALYTICS?

What is Google Analytics?

Google Analytics is a free online tool that helps you to analyze the traffic of your website. Google Analytics is one of the tools that Google is offering and it’s the most popular analytics tool there is. Google Analytics tracks all traffic on your website and combines the data into graphs and diagrams which are easy to review in the clear interface. Google Analytics comes with many integrations from Google Ads to Data Studio. With Google Analytics you’ll be able to evaluate your marketing, content, products and customers. How to use Google Analytics?

Google Analytics collects data of the visitors of your page by using JavaScript, cookies and the browser. The raw data is sent to the Google Analytics server and sorted out for you. On your Google Analytics dashboard you can then see the results in various different metrics and graphs! Google Analytics provides information such as average time on the page, how your page is being used, what are the resources of your traffic, details of your visitors and much more!

It's free!

Using Google Analytics is free. It is suitable for usage from individuals to medium-sized companies. Next to Analytics, Google also offers Analytics 360 package for a monthly fee. Analytics 360 is recommended for larger businesses. Analytics 360 offers more advanced tools to website tracking and access to more detailed data.
